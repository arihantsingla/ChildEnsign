What is Child Ensign?

'Child Ensign' is an assistive pre-school learning resource for auditory impaired children aiming to build an automated system to learn sign language. This tool helps Divyagjan children (People with hearing disabilities) to practice the sign language in more precise way through step by step movement of hands for which sign genration is explained.
